About Me

My name is Marcaus Cruz and I graduated with a Bachelors of Science in Computer Science in December 2021. I was born in Guam, raised in Vancouver, WA, and spent the past 4 years experiencing Bellingham and Seattle WA. I chose to study Computer Science after an intro level class learning C++ intrigued me. At first I loathed the class- it just being another Electrical Engineer pre req. Once we were about halfway through the quarter and I realized how exciting solving these small bugs in these tiny terminal apps were, I made the decision to switch my major. Now, here I am seeking full time employment in a field that I am very passionate in learning more about.
